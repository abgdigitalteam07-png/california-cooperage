Our Heritage

Born in the Wine Country.
Built for the Backyard.

1972 Founded 50+ Years of Craft 3 Rotomold Models

California Cooperage didn't start in a boardroom. It started in Sonoma wine country, where the original cooperage crafted wooden hot tubs for neighbors who wanted something simple — a good soak after a long week.

That spirit never left. Today's CR series keeps it going with rotomold construction, Balboa controls, and no unnecessary frills. Just hot water, good company, and a backyard that finally feels worth coming home to.

Everything You Want to Know

Rotomold means the entire shell is molded from a single piece of polyethylene. No seams, no leaks, and it handles freeze-thaw cycles better than acrylic or fiberglass shells.

All three models ship with Balboa controls — an industry-standard system that is easy to use and widely supported for service.
